Bloons TD 6 (BTD6) is the latest installment and current flagship title of the Bloons Tower Defense series developed and published by Ninja Kiwi. The game was released on June 13th 2018 for Android and iOS, and later to Steam for Windows and Macintosh. Like other Tower Defense games, including those in the BTD series, the premise of Bloons TD 6 is to prevent enemy balloons, referred to as "Bloons", from traversing a track and taking lives.
